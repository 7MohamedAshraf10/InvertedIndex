 If you want to make java at home, you will need some basic equipment and ingredients. You will need a coffee maker or a kettle, a coffee filter or a French press, a coffee grinder or pre-ground coffee beans, and water. You will also need some java mugs or cups to serve your java. To make java, you will need to follow these steps:
Boil some water in your coffee maker or kettle.
Grind your coffee beans to your desired coarseness or use pre-ground coffee beans.
Place your coffee filter in your coffee maker or your French press.
Add your ground coffee to your filter or press according to the ratio of water to coffee that you prefer.
Pour the hot water over the ground coffee and let it brew for a few minutes.
Remove the filter or press the plunger down on your French press.
Pour your java into your mugs or cups and enjoy!
